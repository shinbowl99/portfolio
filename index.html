About me

Hi, my name is Yeil and I am a graduate student from Vancouver Career College.

I was born in Seoul but grew up in the Lower Mainland for most of my life. I joined a media team at my church as a volunteer after graduating high school. I taught myself how to use adobe software to improve the quality of photos and graphics for my church. Eventually, I soon became the go-to person for any design needs. Additionally, I was a youth discipleship leader in the media team for five years and continued to support the youth ministry as staff.

Through the knowledge in graphic design, I am able to leverage my creative skills with photo editing skills and now I spend most days creating websites, editing, and publishing photos on social media platform.
